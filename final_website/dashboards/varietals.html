Instructions

Are you overwhelmed by the variety of wines that you can choose from? Or maybe looking to explore a new varietal that would become your next favorite wine? Here you can explore 19 popular wine varietals from different countries on various dimensions.

     - Sensitive for cost? Look into Average Price
     - Looking for quality? Look into Average Rating
     - Looking for the best bang for the buck? Look into Rating/Price
     - For each varietal, you can also see what countries produce them and how they rank on their ratings
     - Also get a glimpse of what each wine varietal represents by the commonly used words in the description

You can also interact with the charts to track a varietal across different dimensions.
Once you're done exploring, visit the choose a bottle dashboard to pick up a wine of your choice.
