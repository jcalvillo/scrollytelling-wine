Instructions

Are you a wine exporter/import professional who specializes in pinot wines? Or simply want to know more about the industry? Want an at-a-glance market run down to help decide what markets to research?

Left Dashboard: "Market's of Interest"
What retail prices are competitive?
Customers who like pinot category wines will likely try new wines if they are accustomed to a diverse selection. You can use this dashboard to determine which countries have the highest diversity in pinot wines
     Who is likely to try a new pinot wine?
     What's the average price and rating from that country?

Right Dashboard: "Price vs Quality"
What wines compete by price category?
Customers are price sensitive. You can use this dashboard to determine which are the price-ranges of interest in each market
     What are people willing to pay for a pinot wine?
     What's the relationship between price/quality ("value")?
